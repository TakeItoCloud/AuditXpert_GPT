# Governance Model

## Purpose
The governance module maps normalized technical findings into configurable control-domain views, risk register entries, and vCISO-style scorecards. It supports assessment and prioritization; it does not claim automatic compliance certification.

## Outputs
- Framework mapping results per finding
- Risk register records with priority, ownership guidance, and linked findings
- Domain scorecards for executive and fractional CISO style reporting

## Principles
- Keep mappings configurable and versioned
- Preserve evidence references back to technical findings
- Keep control-domain aggregation transparent and reviewable
- Avoid overstating regulatory status or certification outcomes
